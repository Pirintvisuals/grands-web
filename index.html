Emergency Help

What's your situation?

⚡ Urgent — but not dangerous
Before calling, check these quick things:
  1. Check your thermostat is set above room temperature
  2. Check your boiler pressure gauge — it should read 1–1.5 bar
  3. Look for any error codes on the boiler display
  4. Check your gas supply (try another gas appliance)

None of that helped? Call our mobile — 24/7, day or night.

📞 Call 07773 880180 (24/7) WhatsApp us now
🚨 Act immediately
Water damage spreads fast. Do this now:
  1. Turn off your stopcock — usually under the kitchen sink or where water enters the house
  2. Turn off electricity in the affected area
  3. Open taps to drain remaining water from pipes
  4. Call us immediately
📞 Call 07773 880180 (24/7)
🚨 EVACUATE IMMEDIATELY
This is a life safety emergency.
  1. Get everyone out of the building now
  2. Do NOT use any electrical switches (including lights)
  3. Do NOT use your mobile inside the building
  4. Leave the door open as you exit
  5. From outside: Call National Gas Emergency: 0800 111 999 (free, 24/7)
  6. Then call us: 07773 880180
